    Portfolio

    My portfolio is an ever expanding showcase of my talents and experience. My skills include print design, front end web development, photography and brand management.

    I love good clean print and code and I always have both in my projects. I believe that good print is a sharp, quality image - on screen or on paper. Nothing over the top, just subtle effects to effectively & fantastically communicate the message. I encourage the use of dynamically rendered websites, that rely on HTML & CSS and maybe a bit of jquery for fun, rather than images and flash.
